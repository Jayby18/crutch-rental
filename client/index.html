About

'Clutch Crutch' is a crutch rental service based out of Amstelveen. Clutch Crutch also operates in parts of Amsterdam.

Often, people only need crutches for a short time, so they opt to rent one. Clutch Crutch offers a straightforward solution to do so. You just order a crutch (or two) using the easy-to-use order form and the crutch will be delivered on the date specified.

Clutch Crutch offers payment at delivery or after return. So you can focus on getting back on your feet and worry about payment later.
